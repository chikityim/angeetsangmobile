About the digital crafter

Born in Hong Kong but came to Toronto at the young age of 2, Angie is not your typical graphic designer. At a very early age, she always knew her passion was with visual art. When she was just 8 years old, the ambitious child was exposed to her first experience of Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop where it only provoked her passion even more. With her strong attention to details and meticulous personality, it only made sense that she became a digital hand-crafter, precisely crafting and solving problems using graphic art.
